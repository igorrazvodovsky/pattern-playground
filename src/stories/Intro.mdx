## Objective and approach

Using the term *design system* is both helpful and misleading.
Building a design system requires the context of a product or organisation, which this project lacks.
What it resembles more closely is a *design repertoire* â€” a personal collection of patterns that shape how I approach design problemsâ†”solutions.
The project uses design practice itself as the material being organised and explored.

In practical terms the objective is to map relationships between patterns and understand how they connect,
while approaching the process like tending a garden â€” cultivating ideas over time, refining structures and fostering connections.
I'm interested in two layers: patterns as components (the structural building blocks) and patterns as carriers of experiential qualities â€” malleability, agency, accessibility, transparency, and other dimensions of what it feels like to use something.

## On LLMs and AI

I didn't set out to make this an AI project. The plan was to stay technology-neutral. But in reality of 2025 it's become a playground for weaving LLM patterns into component-based structures, using design systems as a lens to explore what's emerging.
