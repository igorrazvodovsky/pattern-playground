# Grouping

Organises a collection of items based on shared attribute values. It structures the representation of
items into clusters, making large datasets more navigable and meaningfully sorted.

Grouping enables users to detect patterns, draw comparisons, or focus on subsets of items that matter
most to their current context or goal.
