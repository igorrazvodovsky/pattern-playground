# Step by step

Navigation follows a designed sequence with clear progression indicators and back/next controls. Flows range from simple two-step processes (search interface ‚Üí results page) to complex multi-stage processes (questionnaires, purchase flows, onboarding), with supplementary options like skip, save and continue later, or jump to step.

## Behavioural position

This model optimises for task completion over exploration, reducing cognitive load from navigation decisions whilst constraining freedom to deviate.

Primarily supports
- [Transactional search](../?path=/docs/foundations-intent-interaction--docs#transactional-search) ‚Äî optimised for getting the job done

Deliberately constrains
- [Exploring](../?path=/docs/foundations-intent-interaction--docs#exploring) ‚Äî intentionally limited to maintain focus
- [Browsing](../?path=/docs/foundations-intent-interaction--docs#browsing) ‚Äî lateral movement discouraged or impossible
- [Navigating](../?path=/docs/foundations-intent-interaction--docs#navigating) ‚Äî constrained to sequence, even when destination is known

## Variants

- Linear: strict sequence, must complete steps in order.
- Flexible: steps shown in overview, some skippable or reorderable. Approaches [pyramid model](../?path=/docs/compositions-structure-pyramid--docs)
- Branching: different paths based on choices or conditions.
- Progressive disclosure sequence: simple initial steps, complexity revealed gradually.
