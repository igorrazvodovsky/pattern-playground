## Related patterns

### Precursors

- [Agency](../?path=/docs/qualities-agency--docs) – enables autonomous result manipulation and improvement actions.
- [Bot](../?path=/docs/activities-bot--docs) – generates the output being presented.
- [Prompt](../?path=/docs/activities-prompt--docs) – the input that leads to generated content.
- [Localization](../?path=/docs/activities-localization--docs) — locale-aware generation

### Follow-ups

- [Conversation](../?path=/docs/activities-conversation--docs) – generated content can become part of ongoing dialogue.
- [Suggestion](../?path=/docs/actions-application-suggestion--docs) – output can inform subsequent suggestions or recommendations.

### Complementary

- [Transparent reasoning](../?path=/docs/activities-transparent-reasoning--docs) – shows the step-by-step process behind the generated content.
- [Explanation](../?path=/docs/actions-sensemaking-explanation--docs) – provides contextual information to clarify AI-driven decisions within the output.
- [AI tuning](../?path=/docs/activities-ai-tuning--docs) – settings that influence how content is generated and presented.
