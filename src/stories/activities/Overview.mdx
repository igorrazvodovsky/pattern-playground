# Activities

Sustained, motive-driven engagements that combine multiple [actions](../?path=/docs/actions-overview--docs) toward larger goals. Activities give actions their meaning — the same action (e.g. searching) serves different purposes depending on the activity it's part of.

## Intelligence

Patterns for working with AI capabilities woven into the interface.

- [Bot](../?path=/docs/activities-bot--docs) — the conversational partner mediating between intent and action
- [Prompt](../?path=/docs/activities-prompt--docs) — expressing what you want the bot to do
- [Conversation](../?path=/docs/activities-conversation--docs) — sustained back-and-forth dialogue, built from conversational [operations](../?path=/docs/operations-overview--docs)
- [Generated content](../?path=/docs/activities-generated-content--docs) — what the bot produces
- [Transparent reasoning](../?path=/docs/activities-transparent-reasoning--docs) — making the bot's thinking visible
- [Embedded intelligence](../?path=/docs/activities-embedded-intelligence--docs) — AI capabilities woven into existing workflows
- [AI tuning](../?path=/docs/activities-ai-tuning--docs) — configuring bot behaviour and preferences

## Collaboration

Patterns for multi-actor coordination around shared artefacts and goals.

- [Collaboration](../?path=/docs/activities-collaboration--docs) — coordination framework for shared work
- [Activity log](../?path=/docs/activities-activity-log--docs) — chronological record of who did what
- [Activity feed](../?path=/docs/activities-activity-feed--docs) — aggregated stream of events

## Learning & growth

Supporting actors in developing expertise and understanding over time.

- [Onboarding](../?path=/docs/activities-onboarding--docs) — first-time experience establishing mental models
- [Help](../?path=/docs/activities-help--docs) — assistance and guidance when needed
- [Mastery](../?path=/docs/activities-mastery--docs) — supporting long-term skill acquisition

## Environments

Sustained contexts in which work happens.

- [Workspace](../?path=/docs/activities-workspace--docs) — unified environment for tasks
- [Living document](../?path=/docs/activities-living-document--docs) — documents that evolve through use
- [Localization](../?path=/docs/activities-localization--docs) — adapting the system to cultural and linguistic context
