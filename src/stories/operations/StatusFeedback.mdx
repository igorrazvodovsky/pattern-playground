# Status feedback

Reflect a change in system state back to the actor, calibrating format and urgency to the information's importance.

The appropriate response varies across three dimensions: the *type* of information being communicated, the *urgency* of the message, and whether the actor needs to take *action*. These dimensions produce three common approaches: validation, notification, and status indication.

## Indication

An indicator is a way of making an element stand out to inform the user that there is something special about it that warrants the user’s attention. Ways to do it include: typographic variations (e.g. making something bold, or changing its colour or size), using icons, and animations.
