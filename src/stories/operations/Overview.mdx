# Operations

Automatic, infrastructural interactions that should just work. Operations are the reliable substrate beneath conscious action — well-established patterns with standardised implementations that actors use without deliberation.

>The boundary between operations and [actions](../?path=/docs/actions-overview--docs) is fluid. An action practised enough becomes operational; an operation in an unfamiliar context demands conscious attention.

## Data entry

Capturing information from actors. These provide the fundamental controls for input with appropriate validation and feedback.

- [Input](../?path=/docs/operations-input--docs)
- [Textarea](../?path=/docs/operations-textarea--docs)
- [Checkbox](../?path=/docs/operations-checkbox--docs)

Input operations combine into [forms](../?path=/docs/actions-application-form--docs) and other compositions.

## Conversation building blocks

Primitives for constructing conversational flows. These form the turn-taking substrate that [conversation](../?path=/docs/activities-conversation--docs) as an activity is built from.

### Conversational activities

Core interaction moves: the actor asking a question through [inquiry](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-conversational-activities-inquiry-user--docs), making an [open request](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-conversational-activities-open-request--docs), the bot doing [extended telling](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-conversational-activities-extended-telling--docs), and the bot asking a question through [inquiry](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-conversational-activities-inquiry-bot--docs).

### Sequence management

Handling breakdowns and transitions within a sequence: [bot repair](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-sequence-management-bot-repair--docs), [user repair](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-sequence-management-user-repair--docs), [sequence completion](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-sequence-management-sequence-completion--docs), and [abort](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-sequence-management-abort--docs).

### Conversation management

Initiating and ending encounters: the bot [opening](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-conversation-management-opening-bot--docs), the actor [opening](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-conversation-management-opening-user--docs), establishing [capability & scope](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-conversation-management-capability-scope--docs), [closing](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-conversation-management-closing--docs), or [disengaging without closing](../?path=/docs/operations-conversation-conversation-management-disengage-without-closing--docs).
