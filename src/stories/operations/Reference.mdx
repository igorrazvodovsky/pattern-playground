# Reference

Surface a linked entity inline, letting the actor verify a connection without navigating away. References share the rhetorical intent of figure-ground-voice linking — compress context, trust the reader — but resolve it through *inline* surfacing rather than navigation. Same move, different interaction mechanism. See [Content § Link rhetoric](../?path=/docs/foundations-content--docs) for the authored-link counterpart.
