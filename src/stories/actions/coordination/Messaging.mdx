# Messaging

The exchange of messages between actors in real time or asynchronously, balancing conversational immediacy with the persistence of shared record. Messaging serves as the primary surface for [conversations](../?path=/docs/activities-conversation--docs) between people and [machines](../?path=/docs/activities-bot--docs).
