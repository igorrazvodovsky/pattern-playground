## Related patterns

- [Popover](../?path=/docs/operations-popover--docs) — underlying positioning and light-dismiss behaviour
- [Bubble menu](../?path=/docs/actions-coordination-bubble-menu--docs) — adds selection-awareness; dropdown provides the container, bubble menu determines *what* goes in it based on content selection
