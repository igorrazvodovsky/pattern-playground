# Actions

Conscious, goal-directed interactions. Actions require the actor's focal awareness — they know what they're trying to achieve and deliberately choose how to get there.

Actions are organised by *lifecycle stage*: where the actor is in their journey from initial exploration to coordinated output. The stages aren't strictly sequential — actors move between them fluidly, and many patterns serve multiple stages. [Filtering](../?path=/docs/actions-seeking-filtering--docs), for instance, supports both initial seeking and later refinement during sense-making.

## Seeking

Finding and accessing information. The actor has intent but may not yet know exactly what they need.

- [Searching](../?path=/docs/actions-seeking-searching--docs) — query-driven retrieval
- [Filtering](../?path=/docs/actions-seeking-filtering--docs) — narrowing scope during exploration
- [Sorting](../?path=/docs/actions-seeking-sorting--docs) — ordering for pattern recognition
- [Data view](../?path=/docs/actions-seeking-data-view--docs) — structured collections supporting browsing
- [Item view](../?path=/docs/actions-seeking-item-view--docs) — detailed examination of individual entities
- [Command menu](../?path=/docs/actions-seeking-command-menu--docs) — quick access to actions and navigation
- [Progressive disclosure](../?path=/docs/actions-seeking-progressive-disclosure--docs) — revealing complexity gradually
- [Dynamic hyperlinks](../?path=/docs/actions-seeking-dynamic-hyperlinks--docs) — contextual navigation within content

## Evaluation

Assessing and comparing what's been found. The actor has located candidates and needs to judge relevance, quality, or fit.

- [List](../?path=/docs/actions-evaluation-list--docs) — scannable sequences for comparison
- [Table](../?path=/docs/actions-evaluation-table--docs) — structured multi-attribute comparison
- [Timeline](../?path=/docs/actions-evaluation-timeline--docs) — temporal sequence and progression
- [Focus and context](../?path=/docs/actions-evaluation-focus-and-context--docs) — balancing detail with situational awareness
- [Text lens](../?path=/docs/actions-evaluation-text-lens--docs) — viewing content through different perspectives

## Sense-making

Understanding and integrating information. The actor is building mental models, finding patterns, and creating meaning from what they've evaluated.

- [Card](../?path=/docs/actions-sensemaking-card--docs) — contained units for scanning and comparison
- [Annotation](../?path=/docs/actions-sensemaking-annotation--docs) — adding layers of meaning to content
- [Grouping](../?path=/docs/actions-sensemaking-grouping--docs) — clustering for categorical understanding
- [Tag](../?path=/docs/actions-sensemaking-tag--docs) — collaborative categorisation
- [View](../?path=/docs/actions-sensemaking-view--docs) — saved perspectives on information
- [Needs-based view](../?path=/docs/actions-sensemaking-needsbased-view--docs) — contextual presentation aligned with actor needs
- [Explanation](../?path=/docs/actions-sensemaking-explanation--docs) — clarifying system behaviour and decisions
- [Block-based editor](../?path=/docs/actions-sensemaking-blockbased-editor--docs) — modular content assembly

## Application

Acting on understanding. The actor commits to changes — entering data, configuring systems, confirming decisions.

- [Form](../?path=/docs/actions-application-form--docs) — structured input with validation
- [Data entry](../?path=/docs/actions-application-data-entry--docs) — philosophy of frictionless input
- [Dialog](../?path=/docs/actions-application-dialog--docs) — focused modal interaction
- [Drawer](../?path=/docs/actions-application-drawer--docs) — contextual side panel
- [Wizard](../?path=/docs/actions-application-wizard--docs) — guided multi-stage tasks
- [Saving](../?path=/docs/actions-application-saving--docs) — committing changes with appropriate friction
- [Deletion](../?path=/docs/actions-application-deletion--docs) — controlled removal with safeguards
- [Action consequences](../?path=/docs/actions-application-action-consequences--docs) — understanding impact before committing
- [Settings](../?path=/docs/actions-application-settings--docs) — configuration and preferences
- [Suggestion](../?path=/docs/actions-application-suggestion--docs) — system-initiated recommendations
- [Template](../?path=/docs/actions-application-template--docs) — predefined structures for content creation

## Coordination

Orchestrating shared work and managing interface structure. The actor arranges tools, communicates with others, and maintains awareness.

- [Toolbar](../?path=/docs/actions-coordination-toolbar--docs) — contextual actions for the current task
- [Nav bar](../?path=/docs/actions-coordination-nav-bar--docs) — primary navigation structure
- [Bubble menu](../?path=/docs/actions-coordination-bubble-menu--docs) — inline contextual editing
- [Dropdown](../?path=/docs/actions-coordination-dropdown--docs) — revealing options on demand
- [Priority+](../?path=/docs/actions-coordination-priority--docs) — responsive action overflow
- [Messaging](../?path=/docs/actions-coordination-messaging--docs) — conversational exchange
- [Commenting](../?path=/docs/actions-coordination-commenting--docs) — discussion around artefacts
- [Notification](../?path=/docs/actions-coordination-notification--docs) — keeping actors appropriately informed

## Navigation

Structural models describing how screens, pages, or spaces connect to one another. These cut across lifecycle stages — some models serve seeking, others evaluation or application. See the [navigation overview](../?path=/docs/actions-navigation-overview--docs) for a decision tree.

- [Flat navigation](../?path=/docs/actions-navigation-flat-navigation--docs), [hub and spoke](../?path=/docs/actions-navigation-hub-and-spoke--docs), [multilevel tree](../?path=/docs/actions-navigation-multilevel-tree--docs), [pyramid](../?path=/docs/actions-navigation-pyramid--docs), [fully connected](../?path=/docs/actions-navigation-fully-connected--docs), [hybrid](../?path=/docs/actions-navigation-hybrid-patterns--docs)
- [Overview and detail](../?path=/docs/actions-navigation-overview-and-detail--docs) — coordinated macro and micro views
- [Pan and zoom](../?path=/docs/actions-navigation-pan-and-zoom--docs) — spatial navigation at different scales
- [Step by step](../?path=/docs/actions-navigation-step-by-step--docs) — prescribed sequences with guided progression
