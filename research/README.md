## research/

Per-topic retrieval notes produced by the `/research` skill. Committed but not canon.

- Canon lives in `references/` and is indexed by `docs/references.md`.
- This folder is a holding area: drafts of design implications, retrieved from Semantic Scholar and synthesised against a specific story's context.
- Structure: `research/<slug>/query.yml` (persistent query) + `research/<slug>/<YYYY-MM-DD>.md` (per-run synthesis).
- Promotion from here into `references/` is a human act. Failed runs stay too — the trail of what wasn't worth keeping is itself information.

See `.claude/skills/research/SKILL.md` for the pipeline.
